About

Bro Bro's 9 is named after the Harrison family, the founders of the company. The Harrison family consists of 9 boys who all help to run the business.

The "9" in Bro Bro's Nine:
Stands for the 9 brothers that operate the business
"Bro Bro's":
Named because it's a family, specifically a brother, ran operation!
